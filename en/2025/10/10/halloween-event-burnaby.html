🎃 Burnaby Halloween Events Guide 2025

October is here — and that means Halloween fun all across Burnaby!
From family-friendly festivals to teen and community events,
here’s a complete list of Halloween happenings in Burnaby, organized by date.

🎫 Most events are free, but a few may require advance registration or small admission fees.

📅 Events by Date

🧡 Tips for Enjoying Halloween in Burnaby

  • 🎭 Dress up!
    Most events encourage costumes — it makes the experience way more fun (and your photos way cuter).

  • 🕓 Know which events need tickets.
    Haunted Village is the only one requiring paid advance tickets.
    Everything else is free and open to the public.

  • 🚗 Plan parking or take transit.
    Weekend crowds can be heavy; arriving early or using SkyTrain is recommended.

  • 📸 Bring your camera.
    Brentwood, Lougheed, and Metrotown malls go all-out with decorations — perfect for family photos!

🎃 Final Thoughts

Throughout October, Burnaby turns into a city-wide Halloween playground.
With so many malls, community centres, and parks joining the fun,
you can celebrate the spooky season without leaving town.

If you’re looking for family-friendly Halloween events this year,
Burnaby has everything you need — all in one city.
